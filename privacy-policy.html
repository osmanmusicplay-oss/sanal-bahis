Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

We value the privacy of users of Sports Betting Game. This policy explains what data is processed and how it is used.

1. Data We Process

Sports Betting Game is an entertainment simulation app that uses only virtual currency. The app:

  • Does not collect real-money, credit card, or payment information.
  • Processes username and technical account/authentication data (such as system account identifiers) for account creation and sign-in.
  • Stores gameplay data linked to your account (such as balance, coupons, and task/streak progress).
  • May process anonymous/technical usage data to improve app quality (including Google Play related services).

2. How We Use Data

Data is used only to operate your account, provide game features, detect errors, and improve app quality. Your data is not sold to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use Supabase for backend/authentication and Google Play Services components for distribution/analytics related features. Data handling by these providers is subject to their own privacy terms.

4. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly process personal data of users under 18.

5. Not Real Gambling

Sports Betting Game uses only virtual currency. No real-money deposit, withdrawal, gain, or loss is possible. The app is for entertainment purposes only.

6. Account Deletion and Data

You can request account deletion through in-app settings. After deletion, account-linked data becomes inaccessible or is processed according to technical/legal requirements.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version published on this page is the valid one.
